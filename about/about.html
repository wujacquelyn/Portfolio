I am Jacquelyn Wu, a Canadian designer, and illustrator that is currently studying BFA Communication Design at Parsons School of Design in New York City. Zealous for everything whimsical and obscure, I aim to narrate a story and provide a voice for my subjects. I enjoy challenging myself by exploring different media and incorporating my illustrations into design pieces.
