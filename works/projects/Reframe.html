Reframe App : Marketing & In-app Motion Graphics

Reframe App (YC 2021) is an alcohol reduction app that helps casual drinkers build healthier habits through neuroscience.

During my time as the first motion design hire, I created highly successful social advertisement campaigns, visualized brand identity, and animated in-app functions.

Motion Logo Identity

Inspired by the brand's tagline of "Reframe the way you think," the logo animation captures this concept by reconstructing the brain logo through fluid movement.

App Promotional Videos & Product Hunt

Collaborating with the Creative Director, I made lottie web animations for Reframe's official website.
Additionally, I executed a product demo video for essential in-app functions for users and advertising. I also self-directed and produced the introduction video for Reframe App's feature video on Product Hunt, a website that surfaces the latest new and upcoming test products.

I conceptualized and created a series of in-app meditation graphics. These include EFT tapping guides, Breathwork, Box Breathing, and Urge Surfing Exercise animations.
