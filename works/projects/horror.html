Eureka: Horror Comic

The following are a few conceptual splash pages of a horror comic. The pages were drawn with a pen-nib, scanned, and then retouched digitally. I based this story on my personal experience of losing a pet to an animal abuser. The plot is that a man approaches and enters a club, intending to pick up his "sister" that needs a ride home. He is surprised to find out that the hosts of the clubs are all— cats? As the plot unfolds, the man is hit with bolts of déjà vu. He realizes that the cats seem to recognize him and are passive-aggressively resentful towards him. Eventually, he realizes that he is a cat abuser that had brutally murdered all these cats, his "sister" was one of the cat owners, and the cats get their revenge through tearing him to shreds.
