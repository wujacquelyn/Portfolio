Luna Lovegood Posters

For this assignment, we were supposed to make four cohesive poster designs that are based of a person or character. Being an avid Harry Potter fan, I took this chance to create four for Luna Lovegood, my favorite character in the series.

Final Iteration

I went with a pink color palette that contrasted with grey. Also, I played around with textures and incorporated illustrations along with typography.

Four posters combined as one.
JACQUELYN WU
