I rummaged through my apartment...

This was another creative exercise during the COVID-19 quarantine. The objective was to create a visualized audio where all elements were intuitively self-generated. No outside source was allowed. First, I found objects I had around my apartment that might inspire certain "sounds." Then, I created the notation digitally via Garageband. After that, I also was able to choose from sounds that others had generated themselves and created a remix, which I then used to create the visual.

The audio that I composed based of the "impressions" that the objects gave me.

The Visualization

My remix of these sounds turned out to have a rather dreamy, ephemeral, and solemn tone. The visualization aimed to not only be in sync with music's tempo but to capture the overall feeling it evoked.
